DORMANT SEASON(WINTER)

TIMING

Late winter (February to early March) is the best time for pruning apple trees.
Prune before new growth begins, but after the coldest part of winter has passed.

BENEFITS

Minimizes stress on the tree.
Allows you to see the tree's structure more clearly.
