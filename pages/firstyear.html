Initial Fertilizer Application

1.Use balanced fertilizer: Choose a 10-10-10 or 12-12-12 fertilizer
2.Apply during planting: Mix fertilizer into the soil at the planting site

Early Growth Support

1.Apply nitrogen-rich fertilizer in early spring: Use a high-nitrogen fertilizer to promote leaf and shoot growth.
2.Avoid excessive nitrogen: Prevent weak, leggy growth by following recommended amounts.

Mid-Season Nutrient Boost

1.Use slow-release fertilizer in late spring: Apply slow-release pellets to provide steady nutrients.
2.Ensure balanced N-P-K ratio: Choose a fertilizer with balanced nutrients for overall health

Late Season Maintenance

1.Apply low-nitrogen fertilizer in late summer: Focus on phosphorus and potassium for root and fruit development.
2.Focus on phosphorus and potassium: Support strong roots and future fruit production
